We invite presentations on computing education and research as well as on efforts to increase diversity in computing, e.g. outreach programs or programs to engage students outside the classroom. Below are the presentation categories:

Education and Research in Computing&

  • Computing Education Talks
  • Computing Research Talks

  • Research Posters or Demos
  • Diversity Presentations
  • Diversity Posters
  • Panel Presentations
  • Birds-of-a-Feather Sessions
